## Profile

Update your personal information to keep your account up to date.

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Name | Your full name, shown in meetings and emails |
| Email | Your login email (cannot be changed) |
| Company | Your business or organization name |
| Role | Your title or role (e.g., Founder, CEO) |

To edit your profile, go to **Settings > Account > Profile** and tap any field to update it.

## Proactive Agent

When **Proactive Agent** is enabled, Kontor actively monitors your integrations and suggests actions without being asked. Examples include:

- Follow-up reminders for unanswered emails
- Meeting prep briefs generated before calls
- Suggested tasks based on recent conversations

When disabled, Kontor only responds to direct requests in Chat.

<Note>Proactive Agent is available on Pro and Scale plans.</Note>
