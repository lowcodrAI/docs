## What's Tracked

Kontor tracks the following usage metrics for your account:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Messages sent | Messages you send in Chat |
| Decisions processed | Items approved, declined, or snoozed in the Decision Queue |
| Routine runs | Automated routines executed by the Proactive Agent |
| Storage used | Files, documents, and meeting recordings stored |
| Documents generated | PDFs, spreadsheets, presentations, and other files created |
| Meeting recordings | Meetings recorded via the notetaker bot |

## Billing Cycle

Usage resets monthly on your billing date. To check your current usage, go to **Settings > Usage**.

## Usage Limits by Plan

| Feature | Free | Solo | Pro | Scale |
|---------|------|------|-----|-------|
| Messages | 50/mo | 500/mo | 2,000/mo | 10,000/mo |
| Decisions | 10/mo | 100/mo | 500/mo | Unlimited |
| Storage | 100 MB | 1 GB | 5 GB | 25 GB |
| Recordings | 0 | 10/mo | 30/mo | 100/mo |
| Proactive Agent | No | No | Yes | Yes |

<Warning>When you hit a usage limit, that feature is paused until your next billing cycle. Upgrade your plan for higher limits.</Warning>
