## How OAuth Works

Connecting a tool takes under 30 seconds:

1. **Tap Connect** on the integration you want.
2. **Sign in** to the service (Gmail, Slack, etc.) in the browser.
3. **Grant permissions** — Kontor only requests the scopes it needs.
4. **Done** — the connection is live and ready to use.

Your OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest and scoped to the minimum permissions required. Kontor never stores your passwords.

<Tip>
You can connect multiple accounts for the same tool — for example, personal and work Gmail — and Kontor will manage them separately.
</Tip>
