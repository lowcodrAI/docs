## What Kontor Can Do

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Draft and send emails** | Compose replies and new messages in your voice. |
| **Summarize unread messages** | Get a quick digest of what's in your inbox. |
| **Create follow-up reminders** | Never forget to reply — Kontor tracks open threads. |
| **Search your inbox** | Find emails by sender, subject, date, or content. |

## How to Connect

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations** in the Kontor app.
2. Tap **Gmail** or **Outlook**.
3. Sign in and grant Kontor permission to read and send email.
4. Your inbox is now connected.

<Tip>
Email drafts always appear in your Decision Queue for approval before sending. Kontor never sends anything without your explicit approval.
</Tip>
