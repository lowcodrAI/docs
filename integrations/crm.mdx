## What Kontor Can Do

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="HubSpot">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Manage contacts** | Create, update, and search contacts and companies. |
    | **Track deals** | View deal pipeline and update stages. |
    | **Marketing** | Access campaign data and engagement metrics. |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Salesforce">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Manage accounts** | Create and update account records. |
    | **Track leads** | View and qualify incoming leads. |
    | **Manage opportunities** | Update deal stages and close dates. |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Pipedrive">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Manage deals** | Create deals and move them through your pipeline. |
    | **Track contacts** | Maintain contact records and activity history. |
    | **Pipeline activities** | Log calls, emails, and meetings against deals. |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How to Connect

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations** in the Kontor app.
2. Tap the CRM you use.
3. Sign in and grant Kontor access to your CRM data.
4. Your contacts, deals, and pipeline data are now available.

<Tip>
Kontor can automatically log meeting notes and follow-ups to your CRM after calls — connect both your calendar and CRM to enable this.
</Tip>
