## What Kontor Can Do

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Slack">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Send messages** | Post to channels or DM teammates. |
    | **Manage channels** | Create, archive, and organize channels. |
    | **Create reminders** | Set Slack reminders for yourself or others. |
    | **Search conversations** | Find messages across your workspace. |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Discord">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **View profile** | Access your Discord profile information. |
    | **Server information** | Browse servers and channel details. |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How to Connect

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations** in the Kontor app.
2. Tap **Slack** or **Discord**.
3. Sign in and authorize Kontor for your workspace or account.
4. You're connected.

<Tip>
Kontor can send Slack messages on your behalf — always with your approval via the Decision Queue. Nothing goes out without you swiping right.
</Tip>
