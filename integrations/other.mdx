## What Kontor Can Do

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Stripe">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **View payments** | Check recent transactions and payment history. |
    | **Manage subscriptions** | View subscriber status and plan details. |
    | **Customer management** | Look up and update customer records. |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="DocuSign">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Send for signature** | Route documents to recipients for signing. |
    | **Track status** | Monitor which documents are pending, signed, or declined. |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Twitter / X">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Post tweets** | Draft and publish posts (with your approval). |
    | **Manage interactions** | View mentions and engagement. |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Instagram">
    | Capability | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Manage posts** | Draft captions and schedule content. |
    | **View interactions** | Monitor comments and engagement. |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How to Connect

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations** in the Kontor app.
2. Tap the service you want to connect.
3. Sign in and authorize Kontor.
4. The integration is ready to use.

<Tip>
Social media posts and Stripe actions always go through the Decision Queue — Kontor will never post or modify data without your approval.
</Tip>
