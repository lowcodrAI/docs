## Annual Billing

Save **20%** by choosing annual billing on any paid plan. Annual pricing is applied at checkout and renews automatically each year.

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) |
|------|---------|--------------------|
| Solo | $19/mo | $15.20/mo |
| Pro | $59/mo | $47.20/mo |
| Scale | $249/mo | $199.20/mo |

<Tip>Start with the Free plan to explore Kontor, then upgrade when you need more capacity.</Tip>

## Upgrade Your Plan

Ready for more? Go to **Settings > Billing > Change Plan** to upgrade. Your new plan takes effect immediately.
