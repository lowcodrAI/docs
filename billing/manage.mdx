---

## Upgrade Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to **Settings > Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a new plan">
    Tap **Change Plan** and select the plan you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and pay">
    Complete checkout. Your new plan takes effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downgrade Your Plan

Follow the same steps as upgrading, but select a lower plan. Downgrades take effect at the **end of your current billing cycle** — you keep full access until then.

<Warning>Downgrading may reduce your usage limits. If you're over the new plan's limits, some features will be paused.</Warning>

## Billing Portal

Manage payment methods, view invoices, and update billing details through the Stripe billing portal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to **Settings > Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open billing portal">
    Tap **Manage Billing** to open the Stripe portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your information">
    Add or change payment methods, download invoices, or update your billing address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancel Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time. When you cancel:

- Access continues until the end of your current billing period.
- Your account reverts to the Free plan.
- Your data is retained — you can resubscribe at any time to regain access.

To cancel, go to **Settings > Billing > Manage Billing** and select **Cancel Plan**.
