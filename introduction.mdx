Kontor is a hosted AI assistant for solopreneurs and small businesses. It connects to your tools (Gmail, Calendar, Slack, Notion, and 25+ more), understands your work, and takes action on your behalf — with your approval. No API keys to manage, no complex setup. Just sign up, connect your accounts, and let Kontor handle the busywork.

## What can Kontor do?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Decision Queue" icon="inbox">
    Kontor surfaces drafts, proposals, and follow-ups for your review. Swipe right to approve, left to edit, up to snooze. You stay in control while Kontor does the heavy lifting.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Chat" icon="message-circle">
    Ask Kontor anything. Generate documents, draft emails, get summaries — all in natural conversation. It remembers your preferences and gets smarter over time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Meeting Copilot" icon="calendar">
    Auto-syncs your calendar, generates prep briefs before meetings, records with consent, and summarizes key takeaways and action items after.
  </Card>
  <Card title="25+ Integrations" icon="plug">
    Gmail, Slack, Notion, Linear, HubSpot, GitHub, and more. Connect any tool in one click with OAuth — no API keys required.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
